APPLE RISOTTO

Apple risotto is a sweet twist of the traditionally savoury Italian recipe

This dish was made popular in recent years by the Japanese anime "Food Wars! : Shokugeki No Soma and the game Bloodstained." The breakfast meal was made to rejuvenate the eater. Apple risotto is a speciality in Trentino South Tyrol, a region in Northern Italy

Ingredients

  • 1 cup arborio rice
  • 1 apple (honey crisp or fuji)
  • 1 small yellow onion
  • 2 tbsp. butter
  • 1/3 cup white wine
  • 1 tbsp. lemon juice
  • 4 cups of apple juice
  • Bacon
  • Italian parsley, fresh
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Apple Risotto Method!

  1. Carefully dice your onions,
    • Slice a few horizontal levels
    • Then make a few long vertical slices making sure to never cut all the way through.
    • From here cut the onion down from the side.
  2. Peel the apple (make sure to kiss it as well).
  3. Cut the apple into cubes and set it aside.
  4. Squeeze some lemon juice on top.
  5. Heat the apple juice to a simmer in a pot.
  6. Melt the butter in a seperate pan over medium heat.
  7. Add in the onions and saute until translucent.
  8. Add the rice and toast for about one minute.
  9. Deglaze the rice with white wine.
  10. Once the alcohol has evaporated, 1/3 cup at a time and stir occasionally.
  11. Add more juice as soon as the rice has absored it in and repeat
  12. After 4 cups once the risotto is soft and tender, add the fresh apple cubes!.
  13. Sprinkle in Parmesan cheese and let it melt.
  14. In a seperate pan, cook up two strips of bacon.
  15. Add the risotto to a plate and layer the strips of bacon on top.
  16. Sprinkle on fresh Italian parsley and pepper! (add msg as well)
