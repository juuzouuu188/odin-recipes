APPLE RISOTTO

Apple risotto is a sweet twist of the traditionally savoury Italian recipe

This dish was made popular in recent years by the Japanese anime "Food Wars! : Shokugeki No Soma and the game Bloodstained." The breakfast meal was made to rejuvenate the eater. Apple risotto is a speciality in Trentino South Tyrol, a region in Northern Italy

Ingredients

  • 1 cup arborio rice
  • 1 apple (honey crisp or fuji)
  • 1 small yellow onion
  • 2 tbsp. butter
  • 1/3 cup white wine
  • 1 tbsp. lemon juice
  • 4 cups of apple juice
  • Bacon
  • Italian parsley, fresh
  • Salt and pepper to taste
