Chaliapin Steak Don

This is a delicious bowl of shiso and umeboshi flavored rice that contains marinated steak too.

Ingredients For Donburi

  • 270 grams steak
  • 350 grams onions (finely diced)
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • Black pepper
  • 1 tablespoon oil
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1/3 cup red wine
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 2 rice cooker cups rice
  • 70 grams umeboshi(10%) (pitted and minced)
  • Scallions for garnish

Chaliapin Steak Don Method!

  1. Finely dice your onions.
  2. Throw them into a bowl,sprinkle on the salt and mix it.
  3. Make a bed of onions on a tray.
  4. To prep the steak, cut some lits into it about one quarter apart and one third of the thickness deep.
  5. Turn over the steak and repeat the process at the same angle.
  6. Set it on the bed of onions and bury it under more onions.
  7. Cover this up and leave it in the fridge overnight.

Umeboshi Prep

  1. Remove the pits from the umeboshi.
  2. Once they are all pitted, use your knife to mince and make a paste.

Cooking Method

  1. Add oil to a hot pan over medium heat.
  2. Add the steak.
  3. Let it cook undisturbed untill it is cooked about one third of the way up the sides.
  4. Flip it over and make sure its done mediun rare.
  5. Take the steak and let it rest somewhere else.
  6. Saute the onions untill they are tender and translucent.
  7. Idea is to get them a little brown.
  8. Add the soy sauce untill and let it cook untill there is no liquid left.
  9. Glaze the pan with the red wine.
  10. Once it no longer smells like alcohol, add the butter and whisk it.
  11. Add the umeboshi into fresh rice and distribute it evenly.
  12. Put it into a bowl.
  13. Slice the steak.
  14. Line it over the rice.
  15. Add the onion sauce on top like a mountain.
  16. Add scalions on top.
